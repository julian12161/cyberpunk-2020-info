    Subject: The Dangers of Night City

    Classification: Civilian Advisory

    Night City is a high-risk urban zone, and all residents are advised to exercise caution when navigating its streets. Below is a breakdown of known threats, including criminal organizations and individuals operating beyond legal parameters.
