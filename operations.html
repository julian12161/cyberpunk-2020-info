    Current Operation Overview

    When a university professor learns that his father figure killed his parents, he plans revenge upon his company using the very virus he had been employed to design. This virus, designed to create sleeper agents for Biotechnica, was now having the effect of creating bozos around the city. Fearing infection himself, the professor leaves the virus on the biotechnica page and goes into hiding.
