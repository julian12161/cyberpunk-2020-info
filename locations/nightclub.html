    Nightclub

    DATATERM DISPLAY SYSTEM

    Topic: Nightclub
    UP

    Vortex

    The bass hits like a body blow the moment you step in—lights strobe violently, bathing the crowd in seizure-inducing reds and greens. The air reeks of sweat, synth-drugs, and ozone, thick enough to choke on. Gangers lounge in shadowed booths, eyes flickering with chrome and chems, while backroom deals happen in plain sight.
    The bartender’s new, too chromed for just pouring drinks. His arms are plated, jaw wired tight, eyes darting between the crowd and the door like he’s waiting for something to go wrong. He’s muscle, barely holding it together.
    Upstairs, in a VIP booth shrouded in red mist and holo-curtains, Aza Vega watches her kingdom. She’s more machine than woman, glimmering with high-end augments and a thousand-yard stare. High as orbit and twice as dangerous, she protects her staff with obsessive loyalty—and won’t hesitate to kill if you give her a reason.
