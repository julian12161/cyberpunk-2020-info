    Blueline Warehouse

    DATATERM DISPLAY SYSTEM

    Topic: Blueline Warehouse
    UP

    Warehouse 47

    Main Map Zones

    1. Exterior
      • Fenced lot with sliding vehicle gate (DV 20 Electronic Security) to bypass quietly.
      • Two patrol drones (“Pseudo Dragons”) orbit perimeter — SP 2, 4 SDP, records video, -2 to hit.
      • Guard shack with one bored cop watching a flatvid (Awareness/Notice -4 to spot intruders). Internal system can open locks and access cameras (Interface check).
    2. Interior
      • Main Floor: Stacked crates/pallets, forklifts, 2 uniformed cops unloading boxes from a delivery truck.
      • Armory Cage: Locked steel mesh (DV 25 Electronic Security, DV 20 w/ tools). Contains:
        • 6x NCPD-issue Patrol Armor (SP 18 legs, SP 20 torso, SP 15 arms, Enc 2)
        • 6x Traffic Helmets (SP 15, two-way radio)
        • 6x NCPD General Purpose Shields (SP 10)
      • Back Office: Desk, outdated computer, paper manifests (DV 15 search, DV 18 hack). Holds:
        • Transaction logs with buyer names (Victor Serrano, Lucio Ramires)
        • Security cam footage of uniformed cops making the sale.
      • Break Room: Two off-duty cops playing cards, distracted. Armed with pistols (2d6+1), Militech Military/Police shotgun (acc 0, conc. N, 4d6, 6 rounds, 2 ROF, ST rel.) in locker.
    3. Possible Player Choices
      • Stealth Hit: Bypass security and take the gear quietly.
      • Smash & Grab: Full frontal assault, consequences from both Saints and NCPD.
      • Frame Job: Steal the goods but plant false evidence pointing to another gang.
      • Buy Out: Approach Serrano to purchase gear directly — risk Lucky’s wrath if word gets back.
