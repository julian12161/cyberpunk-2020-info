    Neonmart

    Fluorescent tubes buzz overhead, flickering in sickly greens and purples that make the whole place feel radioactive. Cheap plastics and chipped metal line the shelves, overflowing with overstocked synth-meat cans and off-brand stimulants. An automated voice loops overhead, pitching "Hot Deals on Brainburner Cola!" while a gang of teenage punks loiter by the discount neuralware, their LED eyes flickering with malice. The air stinks of engine grease, sweat, and burnt soy.
