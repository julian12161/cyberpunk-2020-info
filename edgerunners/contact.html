    Contacts

    DATATERM DISPLAY SYSTEM

    Topic: Edgerunners: Contacts
    UP

    Capability

    Definition: Measures the contact's skill and effectiveness in their occupation.

    Levels and Costs





    Usage: The referee rolls 1D10 and adds the contact's Capability Number to see if they can help with a task.

    Reputation

    Definition: Indicates the contact's influence and connections in their field.

    Levels and Costs





    Application: Determines how well-connected and influential the contact is.

    Availability

    Definition: Measures how often and easily the contact can be reached.

    Levels and Costs





    Contacting: The method of contacting (phone, Net, meeting place) and willingness to help. A LUCK-based roll determines availability, made once per day.

    Reliability

    Definition: Measures how trustworthy and dependable the contact is.

    Levels and Costs





    Trust issues: Reflects the possibility of failure, deception, or betrayal.
